About Me

Adeoluwa Moses Tinuoye: QA Engineer Based in Brandenburg, Germany, I am a dedicated and experienced AWS Certified QA Engineer with a passion for quality assurance and cloud technologies. With a comprehensive background in the software development life cycle across web, mobile, and client/server applications, I bring a quality-first mentality to every project I undertake. My expertise spans JavaScript/Typescript, SQL, and a wide range of QA and devops tools, including AWS Cloud devops, Selenium WebDriver, Cypress, and more. Since embarking on my professional journey, I have collaborated closely with developers, designers, QAs, devops teams, and product managers, gaining a solid understanding of agile methodologies. My experience has equipped me with a robust skill set in API handling and partner relations, ensuring seamless integration and functionality in diverse tech environments. My most recent role at KLIM as a QA Engineer and Release Owner saw me spearheading quality gates, automated testing, and release coordination efforts, significantly enhancing software delivery and automation coverage. Prior positions at Lottie (Totoli) and Nuri (Ex-bitwala) further honed my abilities in creating QA processes, setting up E2E testing, and leading QA initiatives, contributing to high-quality software releases. I hold a Master of Science in Strategic Business Management from the University of Law Business School, Berlin, and a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the University of Abuja, Nigeria. As an AWS Certified Solutions Architect, I am open to remote opportunities that allow me to leverage my technical skills, collaborative spirit, and continuous improvement mindset.
